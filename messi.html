The Journey

A career that redefined what is possible on a football pitch.

1987
Born in Rosario
Lionel Andrés Messi is born on June 24, 1987, in Rosario, Santa Fe, Argentina.
2000
Joins FC Barcelona
At age 13, Messi and his family relocate to Barcelona. He enrolls in La Masia, Barcelona's famed youth academy.
2004
First Team Debut
Makes his official debut for FC Barcelona at 17, beginning a legendary 21-year association with the club.
2009
First Ballon d'Or
Wins his first Ballon d'Or at age 22, scoring 38 goals and leading Barcelona to a historic treble.
2012
91 Goals in a Year
Breaks Gerd Müller's 40-year record by scoring 91 goals in a single calendar year—a feat still unmatched.
2021
Copa América Champion
Finally lifts the Copa América trophy with Argentina, defeating Brazil in the final at the Maracanã.
2022
World Cup Champion
Leads Argentina to FIFA World Cup glory in Qatar, defeating France in one of the greatest finals ever played.
2023
Inter Miami
Joins Inter Miami CF in MLS, bringing his magic to American football and instantly transforming the league.
